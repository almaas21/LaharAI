Privacy Policy

DreamForge AI (com.lahar.ai)

Effective Date: May 23, 2026

Welcome to DreamForge AI ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect and securely store:

  • Google Account ID
  • Email address
  • Display name

If you choose to use the App as a guest, we generate an anonymous guest ID (e.g., guest_1234567890) that is not linked to any personal identity.

1.2 User-Generated Content

  • Text prompts you enter for AI image and video generation
  • Images you upload for image-to-video or image-to-image features
  • Generation history including prompts used, models selected, and generated content URLs

1.3 Device Information

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Android ID (ANDROID_ID) — used exclusively to bind your Pro subscription to your device and prevent unauthorized sharing
  • App version and build number

1.4 Usage Data

  • Credit balance and daily reset timestamps
  • Ad interaction counts (number of rewarded ads watched)
  • Referral codes and referral activity
  • Subscription status (free or Pro)
  • App settings preferences (e.g., dark mode)

1.5 IP Address

Your IP address may be temporarily logged by our backend server for rate limiting and security purposes. IP addresses are stored in memory only and are not permanently retained.

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Local Storage on Your Device

  • Secure Storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain): Authentication tokens (JWT), Google email, name, and user ID are stored in hardware-backed secure storage.
  • App Preferences (SharedPreferences): Credit balance, generation queue, video history, subscription status, theme preferences, and consent flags are stored locally on your device.

3.2 Server-Side Storage

  • Your generation history (prompts, model names, timestamps, and download URLs) is stored on our backend servers.
  • User records (user ID, credit balance, subscription status) are stored in a SQLite database.

3.3 Security Measures

  • All network communication uses HTTPS exclusively — no cleartext (HTTP) traffic is permitted.
  • Authentication tokens are stored in hardware-backed secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain).
  • CDN download URLs for generated content expire after 7 days.
  • Access to backend servers is restricted and protected by API key authentication.

4. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

4.1 Google Services

  • Google Sign-In: Used for authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing: Used for in-app purchases. Google Payments Privacy Policy
  • AdMob (Google Mobile Ads): Used to display ads to free-tier users. AdMob may collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, and usage data to serve personalized ads. AdMob Privacy Information

4.2 AI Generation Providers

Your prompts and uploaded images are sent to one or more of the following AI providers to generate content:

  • Kie AI (kie.ai): Primary video and image generation backend. Receives prompts, uploaded images, and generation parameters.
  • Pixazo (gateway.pixazo.ai): FLUX-based image generation. Receives prompts and generation parameters.
  • Pollinations (pollinations.ai): Free image generation. Prompts are included in the URL request.
  • Mistral AI (mistral.ai): Prompt enhancement and text processing. Receives user prompts.

4.3 File Hosting

  • tmpfiles.org: Used for temporary image hosting during image-to-image workflows. Images are uploaded and temporarily stored.
  • Cloudflare R2: Generated content is hosted on Cloudflare R2 via presigned URLs that expire after 7 days.

5. Data Retention

  • Generation history is retained on our servers until you delete individual records from within the App.
  • Authentication tokens are stored until you sign out of the App.
  • Local app data (credits, settings, queue) is stored on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data.
  • CDN download URLs expire automatically after 7 days, though the URLs remain stored in your local history.
  • IP addresses used for rate limiting are held in memory only and are not permanently stored.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account data, including generation history stored on our servers.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can sign out of the App at any time, which removes your authentication tokens from local storage. You can also clear all local app data through your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email provided below.
